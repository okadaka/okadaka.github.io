Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Basketball Play Recorder ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our web application.

2. Information Collection and Use

Google User Data

When you log in with Google, we access certain information from your Google Profile (specifically your display name, email address, and profile photo) to personalize your experience and manage your cloud storage settings.

Google Drive Access (Limited Use)

The App requests the drive.file scope. As per Google's restricted scope requirements:

  • The App only accesses files that it has created or that you have explicitly opened with the App.
  • We do no have access to and do not read your other Google Drive files or folders.
  • Your data is stored in your own Google Drive account. We do not store your recordings on our own servers.

Our use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Local Processing

All video recording, playback and expor processing occur locally within your web browser. No media data or recording content is transmitted to our servers for processing. Recordings are only stored in your browser or Google Drive (if logged in)

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth and Google Drive API for authentication and cloud storage. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Security

Since your play data is stored locally in your browser and in your personal Google Drive, you are responsible for the security of your device and your Google account credentials.
