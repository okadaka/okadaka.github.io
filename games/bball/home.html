Basketball Play Recorder

The drag-and-drop digital basketball court for coaches and players.

Diagram Actions in Motion

Illustrate complex basketball plays with a simple drag-and-drop interface. No more confusing arrows and line which make it hard to players to understand sequence of moves and passes.

  • Interactive Court: Drag players to create dynamic sequences. Double click on a target player to pass the ball. Drag multiple players at the same time on a touch pad.
  • Recording & Playback: Capture every movement with voice narration and play it back.
  • Update Narration: Re-record audio for the play as needed.
  • Store in Google Drive: Log-in to Google to save plays in Google Drive. Otherwise, they are saved locally in the borwser temporarily
  • Video Export: Generate high-quality MP4 videos for sharing.
