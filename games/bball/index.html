Instructions

  • Move: Drag players on the court to record their movement.
  • Pass: Click on a player to pass the ball to them.
  • Defenders: Drag "D" from the toolbar to add defensive players.
  • Recording: Press Record, move players, then press Stop to save.
  • Narrate: Record bnew audio for the play - saved a as a new play.
  • Store: Log-in to Google to save in Google Drive. Otherwise, recordings are temporarily saved locally in your browser.
  • Export: Make MP4 video of your play for sharing.
  • Privacy: We never send your recordings or audio to our servers.
